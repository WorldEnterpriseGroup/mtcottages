What to have ready

Practical information helps us move faster.

You do not need to upload sensitive identity documents in the public application. We will tell you if a later, secure step is needed.

  • Target move-in date and desired duration
  • Preferred community or communities
  • Household size, pets, and furnished needs
  • Contact information and a short note about your stay
✓

Privacy first

Do not enter Social Security numbers, full birth dates, bank details, or payment-card information in the application form. Any required screening or payment step will be handled separately and securely.

Fees and payments

Nothing should be a surprise.

If an application fee, background-check fee, deposit, or monthly payment applies to a specific stay, we will disclose it before asking you to pay. Payment links will use secure Stripe Checkout once the applicable account and pricing are confirmed.

Application & screening

We will explain whether a fee applies, what it covers, and when it is due. You can ask questions before proceeding.

Deposits & monthly rent

Approved guests can arrange payments directly through secure checkout links or invoices associated with their stay.
