STAY WITH US

Start with a conversation.

This is an inquiry and application—not a lease, approval, or guarantee of housing. Share what you need and we will follow up with current availability and the right next step.

Please do not enter Social Security numbers, payment-card details, bank information, or other highly sensitive documents.

Questions first? Email stay@mtcottages.com.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

Care that makes furnished living feel easy.

A simple application helps us understand what you need before we talk through actual availability.

Tell us

Share your timing, preferred community, household, and the reason for your stay.

We review

We compare your needs with current homes and the details that make a place a fit.

We follow up

We explain the next step, including any home-specific requirements or screening.

You decide

You can ask questions and make an informed decision before moving forward.
