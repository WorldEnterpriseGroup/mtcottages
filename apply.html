Before you begin

A clear first step.

This is an inquiry and application—not a lease, approval, or guarantee of housing. We will review your information, confirm whether a place may fit, and explain any later screening, deposit, payment, or lease steps securely.

Please do not enter Social Security numbers, full birth dates, payment-card details, bank information, or other highly sensitive documents here.

Questions before applying? Email stay@mtcottages.com.
