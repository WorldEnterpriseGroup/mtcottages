Urgent home issues

For issues that cannot wait but are not life-threatening.

Examples can include a major loss of heat in cold weather, a serious water leak, a lockout, or a security issue. Email stay@mtcottages.com and mark the subject “Urgent maintenance.” If you are not sure whether the situation is dangerous, treat it seriously and call 911.
