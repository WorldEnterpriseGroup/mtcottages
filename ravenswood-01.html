About this home

Ravenswood Character with a Front Porch

Walnut Cottage sits on a settled street in Ravenswood, with a front porch that catches the afternoon light and two bedrooms arranged for comfort. The living room is furnished in a way that makes it easy to actually use — a place to eat, read, or watch something after the day is done. The kitchen has what you need to cook real meals, and the laundry is in-unit.

The porch is the kind of feature that changes how you use a house. Morning coffee, an evening call, watching the street go quiet — it makes a difference during a longer stay. Street parking is easy, and the cottage sits within a short drive of Ravenswood’s main amenities.

This is a place with small-city warmth. It works well for a professional looking for a quiet assignment base or anyone who prefers a front porch over a hallway.

The neighborhood

Small-City Comfort in Ravenswood

Ravenswood is a river town with a steady, grounded pace. Grocery stores, pharmacies, and local dining are all close at hand. Walnut Cottage is on a residential street where neighbors look out for each other, and the drive to larger towns is straightforward. Current guests appreciate how easy the rhythm is here — quiet nights, simple errands, and a community that feels welcoming.
