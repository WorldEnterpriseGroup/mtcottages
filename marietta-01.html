About this home

A Compact, Capable One-Bedroom in Historic Marietta

Frederick House is an efficient one-bedroom on one of Marietta’s quiet, tree-lined streets. The layout is straightforward: a bedroom with natural light, a living area that works for both relaxing and a home office, and a kitchen ready for real cooking. Hardwood floors run through the main space, and large windows keep the rooms bright through the afternoon.

This is the kind of place that works well for a traveling professional or a couple spending a season in the river town. Everything is within reach — coffee, groceries, the riverfront trails — without needing to drive across town. The work-from-home setup is practical, with a dedicated workspace and reliable Wi-Fi.

After a long shift or a day of travel, it is good to return to a place where the lights work, the kitchen is stocked, and the street outside stays quiet. Frederick House is ready for that.
