What we believe

A place can change how a season feels.

When someone arrives for work, relocation, family care, insurance repairs, or a new chapter, the details of the place matter. A real kitchen matters. A quiet bedroom matters. A clean, cared-for home matters.

We bring together furnished studios, apartments, cottages, and houses with a direct, human process. Our guests are not booking a generic room. They are finding a place to live for a while.

Come for what brought you here. Stay because the place lets you settle in.
Our standard

Warm, clear, and well cared for.

The valley we call home

Six communities, one guest-facing welcome.

Our cottages are located across Marietta, Athens, Racine, Parkersburg, Ravenswood, and Grantsville. The places are different; our intention is the same: give guests a comfortable, furnished base from which to work, recover, relocate, study, or build a longer chapter.
