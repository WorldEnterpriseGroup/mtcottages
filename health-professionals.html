REST BETWEEN SHIFTS

Furnished housing built around healthcare schedules

Mt Cottages has hosted traveling healthcare professionals for more than 10 years. We understand that a clinical assignment is not a vacation: you need a dependable place to sleep, prepare meals, do laundry, and reset between demanding shifts.

Our furnished cottages serve Mid-Ohio Valley communities in Ohio and West Virginia. Sleeping areas include blackout curtains to support daytime rest after overnight work. Window treatments and other furnishings can differ by home, so we will confirm the setup of a specific cottage before you commit.

HEALTHCARE EMPLOYERS

Hospitals and care facilities across the region

These are examples of active healthcare facilities in and around the communities we serve, not a complete employer list. Facility links go to the provider or regulator so you can confirm the current campus for your assignment.

Parkersburg and the river communities

WVU Medicine Camden Clark Medical Center is in Parkersburg and operates hospital and specialty services. Marietta Memorial Hospital is across the Ohio River in Marietta, while Memorial Health System also lists clinical locations in Belpre and other nearby communities.

Our Parkersburg and Marietta cottages can keep guests in the same community as some major campuses. The best match depends on the exact worksite, shift route, dates, and current inventory.

Ravenswood and Grantsville-area care

The West Virginia Office of Health Facility Licensure and Certification lists Ravenswood Care Center as an active assisted living residence in Ravenswood. Minnie Hamilton Health System Hospital provides hospital care in Grantsville and the system also operates long-term care.

Ask us which served community is the most practical base for the exact facility named in your contract. Availability in smaller communities can be limited and changes over time.

Skilled nursing and extended care

Healthcare assignments also happen outside hospitals. Harmar Place describes its Marietta community as providing skilled nursing care for short and longer stays. Minnie Hamilton identifies long-term care among its Grantsville services, and the state listing confirms Ravenswood Care Center’s local operation.

If your employer is a nursing home, rehabilitation center, clinic, home-health agency, or specialty practice, include the facility name in your inquiry so we can discuss the most useful community—without publishing a cottage’s private street address.

ROUTE PLANNING

Confirm your exact worksite before choosing a cottage

Hospital systems may operate multiple campuses, clinics, and administrative buildings under one brand. We describe proximity by community because routes vary by worksite, traffic, weather, road work, and time of day. Once you share the facility and department location, we can identify potentially suitable homes; you should verify the route with your preferred mapping service before signing.

ASSIGNMENT-READY COMFORT

Practical features for working clinicians

We focus on the home routines that make a demanding assignment easier. Exact amenities and policies vary, and we confirm them for the cottage you are considering.

  • Blackout curtains in sleeping areas, with the specific setup confirmed before booking
  • A furnished bedroom and living space for off-shift recovery
  • A kitchen for meal preparation around nontraditional hours
  • Laundry access as listed for the individual cottage
  • Internet information available for charting, coursework, and personal use
  • Parking details explained before move-in
  • Direct contact for maintenance and practical stay questions
  • Stay terms discussed around the known assignment dates

HEALTHCARE HOUSING FAQ

What to include in your housing inquiry

Do you rent only to travel nurses?

No. We welcome inquiries from nurses, physicians, residents, therapists, technicians, allied health professionals, healthcare support staff, and others working a temporary or longer assignment. Every applicant and stay is subject to current availability, screening, and the terms for the specific home.

Can you guarantee a commute time?

No. We can explain which cottage community may suit a named campus, but we do not promise drive times. Confirm the worksite—not just the health-system name—and check the route for the time your shift begins and ends.

Can I extend if my contract changes?

Extensions may be possible when the home remains available and both parties agree to updated terms. Tell us as soon as your assignment changes; an extension is not guaranteed until confirmed in writing.

What should I send first?

Share the facility or campus, assignment dates, number of occupants, preferred home size, vehicle count, pets, budget, and any sleep, accessibility, or furnishing needs. Do not send protected patient information.
