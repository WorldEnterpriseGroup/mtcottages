About this home

A Full Three-Bedroom House with Room to Spread Out

Broad Street House is a full three-bedroom home with a larger yard and a layout that works for families, shared schedules, or anyone who needs more than compact quarters. The living room is spacious enough for gathering, and the dining area connects naturally to the kitchen for everyday meals.

Three bedrooms offer real flexibility: a primary suite, a second for children or guests, and a third that works as a home office or hobby room. The full kitchen is equipped for serious cooking — whether that is meal prep for the week or a shared dinner. Parking is included, and the larger yard gives space to step outside without going far.

This is a place where a family in transition, a group of traveling professionals, or anyone needing room to breathe can settle in properly.
