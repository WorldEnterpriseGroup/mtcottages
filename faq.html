Common questions

Good questions make good stays.

Here is the short version. If your question is not answered, email stay@mtcottages.com.

Home / FAQs
What is a mid-term stay?

We use “mid-term” for furnished stays that are generally longer than a short visit and shorter or more flexible than a traditional long lease. Many requests fall in the 30-day to 12-month range; the exact minimum, terms, and availability depend on the home.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Yes. Longer furnished commitments are welcome. Apply with your target duration and we will explain which homes and terms are currently available.

Are utilities and internet included?

It depends on the cottage and agreement. We will identify what is included, what is separate, and what you should expect before you commit.

Do I have to pay to apply?

If an application or background-check fee applies, we will disclose the amount, purpose, and timing before payment. Never send card information through the application form or email.

How do deposits and monthly payments work?

After a stay is reviewed and approved, we can arrange secure payment links or invoices for applicable deposits and monthly payments. The payment method and schedule will be explained with the stay details.

What should I include in my application?

Include your target move-in date, duration, preferred community, household size, pets, furnished needs, and a short description of why you are staying. Do not include Social Security numbers, full birth dates, or payment-card information.

Do you publish every home and street address?

No. Public pages describe our communities and stay types. Specific home details and current availability are shared directly with qualified applicants. Street-level inventory is maintained privately and is not public site copy.

Still wondering?

Ask us directly.

We are happy to talk through the right length of stay and the next step.
