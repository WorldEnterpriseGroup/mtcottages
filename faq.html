What kind of places does Mt Cottages offer?

We offer fully furnished studios, apartments, cottages, and houses. Each arrangement is for the whole private place—not a room in a shared home.

How long can I stay?

We focus on furnished living for 30 days, a season, a year, or longer. Tell us what you expect, even if your timeline is still changing, and we will discuss the best fit.

Do I have to apply before I can stay?

Yes. The public application is an inquiry and the first step in the process. We review your needs and availability, then explain any home-specific screening, lease, deposit, or payment steps securely.

Are all of the places furnished?

Yes. Furnishings are part of the Mt Cottages experience. The exact furniture, kitchen items, linens, utilities, parking, and other amenities vary by place and will be confirmed before you commit.

Can travel nurses and healthcare professionals apply?

Absolutely. Travel nurses are an important part of our guest community. Include your assignment dates, work location, shift or quiet-hours needs, and preferred town in your application.

Can insurance companies place displaced households?

Yes. We welcome conversations with adjusters, claims teams, and placement partners about furnished housing while a household is displaced. Email stay@mtcottages.com to begin.

Can I bring a pet?

Pet policies are home-specific. Tell us about your pet early so we can look for a place where the arrangement is allowed and comfortable.

Where are the exact addresses?

We keep street-level addresses out of the public site. Once we understand your needs and have an appropriate opening to discuss, we can share the relevant details directly.

How do I request maintenance after move-in?

Residents can use the Maintenance Requests page or the resident instructions provided for their home. For an immediate life-safety emergency, call 911 first.

Still wondering?

We would rather answer a question early than have you make a decision without the right information.
