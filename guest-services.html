CLEAR, HUMAN SUPPORT

A direct point of contact before and during your stay

Extended stays come with more questions than a short hotel visit. Mt Cottages provides direct communication to help prospective guests understand available homes, plan an arrival, and know where to turn when a property question arises.

Service begins with accurate expectations. We explain the information available for the specific cottage and route requests to the appropriate person. Response methods, timing, access instructions, maintenance handling, and any add-on service depend on the situation; emergency services should always be contacted first for threats to life or safety.

How we can help

  • Home and location questions
  • Stay inquiry guidance
  • Property-specific arrival details
  • Maintenance request routing
  • Extension or date-change inquiries
  • Amenity clarification
  • Optional service requests
  • Resident resource directions

Before move-in

Tell us who will be staying, why you need housing, your desired community and dates, budget, pets, and any accessibility or furnishing requirements. That information helps the team have a useful conversation about current possibilities. Screening, terms, payment requirements, and approvals are handled as part of the applicable stay process.

While you are here

If something in the home needs attention, report the issue with the property, a clear description, and photos when useful. Maintenance urgency is assessed based on the facts provided. Guests should use emergency services for immediate danger and follow the separate emergency-maintenance guidance for urgent property conditions.

Keep the conversation tied to your actual stay

Availability and home details can change, so save important confirmations and review the terms associated with your cottage. If your dates, occupants, pets, or needs change, contact the team early. A request to modify a stay is not automatically approved and may depend on availability and updated terms.

For self-service resident tasks, use the Resident Portal, maintenance request page, and emergency maintenance page. For a new furnished stay, use the stay inquiry so the team receives the practical details needed to follow up.

Frequently asked questions

  • 01Can guest services reserve a home immediately?

    An inquiry starts the conversation but does not itself create a reservation or guarantee availability. The applicable review and approval steps still need to be completed.

  • 02Who do I contact for maintenance?

    Use the maintenance request process for non-emergency property issues. For immediate danger, contact emergency services first; urgent property situations are explained on the emergency maintenance page.

  • 03Can I extend my stay?

    Ask as early as possible. Extensions depend on availability, the home, and updated stay terms, and are not guaranteed until confirmed.

  • 04Can the team arrange optional services?

    You may ask about services such as meal preparation or housekeeping. Availability, scope, scheduling, and cost must be confirmed for the particular request.

Ready to discuss the details?

Explore current cottage information or send a stay inquiry. We will discuss the home and any requested service based on current availability and the needs you provide.
