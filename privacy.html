Privacy note

Only share what helps us respond.

The public application is designed for an initial rental inquiry. It is not an identity-verification or payment form.

Home / Privacy note

What we collect

When you apply, Mt Cottages receives the contact and housing-preference information you choose to submit, such as your name, email, phone, target dates, desired stay length, preferred communities, household details, and message.

What not to submit

Do not submit Social Security numbers, full birth dates, driver’s-license numbers, bank-account information, payment-card information, medical records, or other sensitive identity documents through this form or ordinary email.

How we use an inquiry

We use the information to review furnished-stay availability, contact you about your request, explain next steps, and coordinate an application or rental conversation. Application information is routed to our internal intake system for follow-up.

Screening and payments

If a specific home requires screening, we will explain the next secure step. If a fee, deposit, or monthly payment applies, we will disclose it before payment and use a secure payment workflow. The public form itself does not process card details.

Questions

For questions about an application or this privacy note, email stay@mtcottages.com.
