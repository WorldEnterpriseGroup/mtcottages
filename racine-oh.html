Racine is a quiet village in Meigs County, Ohio, set along the banks of the Ohio River. For guests who want river-town life with easy access to the wider Mid-Ohio Valley, Racine offers a peaceful setting with a strong sense of community.

About Racine

Located along State Route 124 and the Ohio River, Racine is a short drive from Pomeroy, the Meigs County seat, and within an hour of Marietta, Parkersburg, and Athens. The area offers river access, outdoor recreation, and a pace of life that appeals to guests looking for a genuine small-town setting.
