GRANTSVILLE, WV

Furnished Cottage Rentals in Grantsville

Grantsville is the county seat of Calhoun County, a small community in the heart of the Mid-Ohio Valley. For guests who want quiet, space, and a slower pace while still having access to the wider valley, Grantsville offers a genuine small-town setting.

About Grantsville

Situated along the West Fork of the Little Kanawha River, Grantsville is known for its quiet streets, friendly community, and the annual Calhoun County Wood Festival. The town serves as a gateway to outdoor recreation including hiking, fishing, and river access in the surrounding Calhoun County area.

Guests considering Grantsville typically want a home base near family, a peaceful setting for a longer stay, or proximity to outdoor life in the valley. While the cottage inventory is still growing, we welcome conversations about the area and can discuss available options nearby.
