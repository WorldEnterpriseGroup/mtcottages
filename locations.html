LOCATION GUIDES

Choose the town that fits the life around your stay

A furnished home is only part of the decision. Compare access to work, healthcare, daily shopping, transportation, recreation, and the wider Mid-Ohio Valley before you inquire. Each guide explains the local tradeoffs and identifies only the cottages we can publicly associate with that community.

Cottage availability, amenities, policies, rates, and minimum stays can change. Town services and business hours change too, so our guides link to official local sources where you can confirm current details.

Marietta, Ohio

Historic river-city living with a walkable downtown core, regional healthcare, college and professional activity, and direct access to I-77. A practical option for assignments and longer stays centered on Washington County.

Public cottage selection: Frederick Cottage

EXPLORE MARIETTA

Parkersburg, West Virginia

The region’s largest service center offers broader shopping and dining choices, a local fixed-route bus network, major healthcare, riverfront parks, and convenient routes toward Vienna, Belpre, and Marietta.

Public cottage selection: Broad, Buck Apartment 1, Oak, and Yellow

EXPLORE PARKERSBURG

Ravenswood, West Virginia

A smaller Ohio River community with a traditional downtown, riverfront recreation, US-33 access, and a quieter daily rhythm. Useful for Jackson County work, family visits, and guests who value a close-knit setting.

Public cottage selection: Henrietta, White, and Virginia

EXPLORE RAVENSWOOD

Grantsville, West Virginia

A rural Calhoun County base near local healthcare and outdoor recreation. Planning here calls for an honest look at driving, groceries, weather, mobile service, and the limited nature of small-town inventory.

No Grantsville cottage is currently listed publicly

EXPLORE GRANTSVILLE

Racine, Ohio

Ohio River village life in Meigs County, with local schools, a village park, county demand-response transit, and road connections toward Pomeroy, Athens, Ravenswood, and other regional centers.

No canonical Mt Cottages home is currently listed in Racine
