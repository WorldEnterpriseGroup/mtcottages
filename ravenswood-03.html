About this home

Quiet Comfort with Garden Access

Henrietta Cottage is a two-bedroom house with access to a garden that makes the stay feel settled. The interior is comfortable and straightforward — a living room with enough room to relax, a kitchen with everything for daily cooking, and two bedrooms that offer separation and quiet. The washer and dryer are in the unit, adding convenience for longer stays.

The garden access is what sets this cottage apart. Having outdoor space that is yours to use — for morning coffee, an evening wind-down, or simply looking at something green — changes the experience of an extended stay. Parking is included, and the cottage sits on a quiet street.

This is a good fit for a professional who needs a calm home base, a couple, or anyone who values a bit of outdoor space with their furnished living.
