ARRIVE READY

Furnished housing for work assignments and relocation

A new role or regional project already comes with a full to-do list. Mt Cottages offers furnished homes across Mid-Ohio Valley communities so you can settle into a real kitchen, bedroom, and living space while the longer-term plan comes together.

Our cottages may suit individual professionals, relocating households, contractors, consultants, and project staff. Availability, stay length, screening, pricing, included utilities, and home features vary, so we confirm the full arrangement for the specific cottage before move-in.

ASK ABOUT RELOCATION HOUSING

ONE HOME, MANY TRANSITIONS

A practical base while work is changing

Tell us why you are coming, where you need to be, and what has to work at home. That context helps us discuss current options without assuming every cottage fits every assignment.

New hires and corporate relocation

Start the job without rushing into an unfamiliar long-term lease or buying decision. A furnished cottage can provide a bridge while you learn the area, coordinate a household move, or wait for a permanent home to become ready.

Relocation managers and employers can contact us too. We will explain the available home, proposed terms, documentation process, and who is responsible for payment before anyone relies on an arrangement.

Projects, contracts, and field work

Construction, engineering, manufacturing, energy, public-sector, and consulting projects can bring people to the region for a defined stretch of time. A house gives project staff a place to cook, wash work clothes, and separate the workday from the evening.

For multiple workers, share the number of occupants, preferred bedroom arrangement, vehicles, worksite community, and schedule. Occupancy limits and suitability are evaluated home by home.

Remote and hybrid professionals

If part of your assignment happens online, ask about the internet service and work-surface options in the individual cottage. We can describe the current setup, but bandwidth, cellular reception, desk style, and room layout differ by home.

Bring any employer-required security equipment and confirm that your company permits remote work from the chosen location. Mt Cottages provides housing, not IT support or a guarantee for specialized connectivity requirements.

MOVE WITH LESS SETUP

The everyday pieces are already here

A furnished home reduces the amount you need to purchase and move for a temporary chapter. Review the individual listing and written terms for exactly what is included.

  • Laundry details provided before move-in
  • Space to unpack for a longer stay
  • Community options in Ohio and West Virginia
  • Parking, internet, and utility terms explained for the cottage

  • Possible extension discussions when availability allows

Choose the right home size

Options range by community and availability. Share who is staying so we can discuss an appropriate layout.

A CLEARER MOVE

Plan the stay in four conversations

1. Share the assignment

Provide the worksite community, start date, likely end date, and any on-call or vehicle considerations.

2. Describe the household

Tell us the occupant count, bedrooms needed, pets, accessibility needs, and nonnegotiable features.

3. Review the home

We discuss current availability, confirmed amenities, proposed pricing, screening, policies, and terms.

4. Confirm in writing

A home or extension is not reserved until the required agreement, approvals, and payments are complete.

WORK HOUSING FAQ

Questions professionals and employers ask

Can an employer arrange the stay?

Yes, employers and relocation coordinators may inquire. We still need to identify the occupants, confirm the responsible contracting and paying party, complete any required screening, and document the stay. Employer payment or direct billing is not assumed until accepted in writing.

Can I choose a home by commute time?

Tell us the exact worksite and we can identify communities that may make sense. We do not guarantee drive times because routes change with traffic, weather, road conditions, and shift schedule. Verify the route independently before committing.

What if my project end date moves?

Ask as early as possible. A shorter or longer stay may require revised terms and depends on the agreement and future availability. Do not assume an extension or early departure has been approved until it is confirmed in writing.

Are pets or multiple vehicles allowed?

Policies and space vary by cottage. Include pets, vehicle count, trailers, and any work equipment in the first inquiry so we can avoid suggesting a home that is not suitable.
