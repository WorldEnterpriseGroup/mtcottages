About this home

A Comfortable Two-Bedroom on a Practical Street

Broad Street Cottage is a well-kept two-bedroom house on a practical street in Parkersburg. The living room opens into a dining area that works for shared meals or spreading out with work. The kitchen has full appliances and counter space — enough for cooking during an assignment-length stay. Both bedrooms are furnished with comfortable beds, and the second bedroom works well as a guest room or home office.

The backyard offers some outdoor space, and the off-street parking means one less thing to coordinate. This is the kind of place where a traveling nurse, a relocating professional, or a small family can settle into a rhythm.

Everything you need for daily life is already here. Show up with your bags and your plans. The cottage handles the rest.

The neighborhood

Walkable Comfort in Parkersburg

Parkersburg blends small-city practicality with the convenience of a regional hub. Groceries, dining, and healthcare facilities are all within a short drive. Broad Street Cottage sits on a settled street where neighbors know each other and the morning commute is straightforward. Current guests often note how easy it is to find what they need here — from supplies to a good meal — without losing the quiet of a residential block.
