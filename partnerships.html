PARTNERSHIPS

Care that makes furnished living feel easy.

Mt Cottages can support partner-led housing for visiting scientists, researchers, students, fellows, and other guests whose work brings them to the valley.

INSTAR Research Residences

A partnership pathway for scientists and research professionals who need a furnished place during a project, visit, or period of collaboration.

Curiosity Cottages

Furnished housing for students and fellows who come to the Mid-Ohio Valley for a fellowship, study period, or research experience.

Program Housing
