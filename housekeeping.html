OPTIONAL HOME SUPPORT

Housekeeping that can be shaped around an extended stay

Keeping a home comfortable during a busy assignment, relocation, family transition, or recovery can take time. Mt Cottages can discuss optional housekeeping for eligible stays when staffing, scheduling, property access, and the requested scope align.

Housekeeping is not automatically included with every cottage or stay. Frequency, rooms, tasks, supplies, laundry or linen handling, access, preparation requirements, price, and provider availability must be confirmed in advance. A request does not guarantee that service can be scheduled.

Possible topics for a service plan

  • One-time or recurring visits
  • Kitchen and bathroom cleaning
  • Vacuuming and floor care
  • Dusting accessible surfaces
  • Linen or towel requests
  • Supply expectations
  • Pet and access arrangements
  • Price and cancellation terms

Agree on scope before the visit

Housekeeping can mean different things to different households. Describe the rooms and tasks that matter, the desired frequency, and any surfaces or belongings needing special care. Deep cleaning, dishes, laundry, linen changes, organization, or specialty cleaning should never be assumed unless specifically accepted.

Prepare the home for safe access

Guests may need to secure pets, store valuables, remove personal items from work surfaces, and provide access at an agreed time. The provider may be unable to move heavy items, handle hazardous materials, or clean an area that cannot be accessed safely. Any visit-specific instructions should be shared ahead of time.

Requesting housekeeping

Include housekeeping in your initial stay inquiry when possible, or contact the team during your stay with the desired dates, frequency, and scope. We will review the cottage and available service options, then explain whether the request can be accommodated and what it would cost.

Guests continue to have everyday care responsibilities under their stay terms, including reasonable cleanliness, trash handling, preventing damage, and promptly reporting maintenance concerns. Optional housekeeping supplements those responsibilities; it does not replace them.

Frequently asked questions

  • 01Is housekeeping included with every stay?

    No. It is an optional, inquiry-based service that depends on the home, scope, schedule, staffing, and confirmed terms.

  • 02Can I choose weekly or monthly service?

    You may request a preferred frequency. The available schedule may differ and is not set until the team confirms it.

  • 03Are laundry and linen changes included?

    Only if explicitly included in the agreed scope. Equipment, linens, supplies, and provider capabilities vary.

  • 04Do I need to be home during service?

    Access arrangements are property- and visit-specific. They must be confirmed in advance, along with instructions for pets, alarms, valuables, and areas that should not be entered.
