About this home

A Roomy Three-Bedroom with Room for Family

45th Street House offers a full three-bedroom layout with more room to spread out. The living room is comfortably sized, with enough seating for a family evening or for when colleagues stop by. The kitchen has full appliances, storage, and counter space — suited for real meal preparation during a longer stay.

Three bedrooms mean flexibility: a primary room, a second for a family member or roommate, and a third that works well as an office or guest room. The driveway parking takes the guesswork out of coming and going. The yard gives a bit of outdoor space without requiring much upkeep.

This house works well for a family in transition, a shared assignment schedule, or anyone who needs more than one or two rooms to make the stay work.

The neighborhood

Room to Settle in Parkersburg

Parkersburg is the largest town in the Mid-Ohio Valley, with a full range of grocery stores, healthcare, dining, and retail. The 45th Street location puts you close to the main corridors while staying on a residential street. Guests here appreciate having both space inside and quick access to everything the town offers — errands are simple, and the house itself stays quiet.
